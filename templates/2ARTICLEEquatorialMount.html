Celestron CG-4 German Equatorial Mount and Tripod REVIEW

June 1st by Paul McBurney Jr

By the end of this article, you'll know whether not the Celestron CG-4 German Equatorial Mount and Tripod is worth your purchase.

About

The Celestron CG-4 German Equatorial Mount is an sturdy and reliable mount/tripod manufactured by Celestron.

"One of the best astronomy purchases I have made in a while." - Bart Brown

Celestron is a world famous company in Torrance, California that builds and designs microscopes, telescopes and all sorts of optical technologies.

Reasons to Purchase

Celestron is a well known optical instrument manufacturer. By purchasing their equatorial mount you'll recieve these benefits.

  • 2 year long warranty.

  • 2 counterweights included. (7lb & 4lb)

  • Comes with RA and DDC slow motion control systems.

The German Equatorial Mount can also be compacted down and transported with ease.

Furthermore, the mounts 10 pound weight allows it to be operated and moved with ease. The mount's lightweight attributes means it can be operated by kids that want to get into the astronomy hobby.

Price Range

Overall

Overall, the German Equatorial Mount is a great option for amateur astronomers. It's portablity allows it to be moved with ease. Many mount users say that they often travel with their Celestron Equatorial Mount.
