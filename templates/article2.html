Will an asteroid hit earth soon?


Could this large asteroid hit earth in 2024?

Yes, it could! It's hard to even imagine the disastrous effects and suffering that an asteroid impact could cause..

2007 FT3 (Asteroid) was identified by NASA and classified as an asteroid that could hit earth in 2024. According to NASA's Sentry program, FT3 (Asteroid) is about 340 meters in diameter. (Also 1100 Feet)

Now the question that many are asking is "what would happen if 2007 FT3 hit earth?"

We can use this asteroid impact simulation to see!

Photo From Asteroid Collision Map (An interactive asteroid map/game)

According to the linked simulation, a worst case scenario could cause disastrous effects like skin burns, shockwaves, tsunamis, destroyed infastructure, and deaths.

However, these results are unlikely because most of the world is rural and unpopulated. This means that an asteroid collision with these areas would result in little to no casualties.

Furthermore, 70% of the earths surface is accounted for by ocean. While huge asteroids can cause dangerous tsunamis, 2007 FT3 is not even close to that threat level.



Currently, it is proclaimed that FT3's chance of impact is extremely low. With varying impact percentages it can be impossible to know the true number, however it can be predicted that FT3 most likely not impact earth on October 2nd, 2022.

Even though 2007 FT3 probably won't hit earth, these near misses prove a point that we must be ready for a situation similar to this. With growing concerns that extinction causing asteroids are coming our way, we need to be prepared.

Scientists state that we are 5 million years overdue for a major asteroid collision similar to the one that extincted the dinosaurs.
