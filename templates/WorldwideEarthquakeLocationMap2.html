Steps: Follow the directions below to understand the earthquake location simulation map!

1. First, click the button to show the locations of all earthquakes. Then locate the locations of Earthquakes. These areas are marked with markers with their color depending on severity/magnitude of earthquake.

2. Hover over the location of the earthquake. When you hover over this location, a box will popup giving information about the certain earthquake.

3. After knowing the location of the earthquake, you can hover over the marker and view information about the earthquake like its magnitude, depth, time, and date of impact. All very helpful and insightful statistics, all while knowing the location of the earthquake!
