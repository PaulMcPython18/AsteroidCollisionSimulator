Welcome to Nuke Detonation Location Map. Locate and visualize the locations of all discovered major Nuke Detonations from 1945 to 1988. Nuke Detonations are shown if their magnitude is greater than 6.5! ↓

Click! Click the button below to show the locations of all known major Nuke Detonations greater than 6.5 magnitude.

Steps: Follow the directions below to understand the Nuke Detonation Location Map!

1. First, click the button to show the locations of all Nuke Detonations. Then locate the locations of Nuke Detonations. These areas are marked with markers with their color depending on severity/yield of Nuke Detonation.

2. Hover over the location of the Nuke Detonation. When you hover over this location, a box will popup giving information about the certain Nuke Detonation.

3. After knowing the location of the Nuke Detonation, you can hover over the marker and view information about the Nuke Detonation like its yield, depth, country, and date of detonation. All very helpful and insightful statistics, all while knowing the location of the Nuke Detonation! The recorded time period is from 1945 to 1988.
