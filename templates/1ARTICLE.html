An Honest Review:

Celestron PowerSeeker 127EQ Telescope Review

June 1st by Paul McBurney Jr

By the end of this article, you'll know whether the Celestron PowerSeeker 127EQ Telescope is the right fit for you.

About

The Celestron Powerseeker is an easy to use, but powerful telescope open to the consumer market.

"The Celeston Powerseeker 127EQ is probably on of the best astronomy purchases I have ever made. You get the best for your buck." - Joey Brownsworth

Out of the box, you will recieve several high quality stargazing instruments and one of the best beginner telescopes in the world.

View Celestron Deals on Amazon ←

Why Purchase?

The Celestron Company has years of experience under their belt in building amazing beginner telescopes and products. Here are some benefits from purchasing from Celestron.

  • Best customer support in the telescope industry.

  • Manual German Equatorial Mount Included

  • Compact and portable for easy storage and movement.

  • Two interchangeable eyepieces. (20mm and 4mm)

The Powerseeker 127EQ's lightweight attribute allows it to be used and operated by both kids and adults passionate about astronomy.

Good Price?

For a beginner telescope, the PS 127EQ is about as good as you'll get in a moderate price range.

At the time of publication, the PowerSeeker costs just south of $149.99. An amazing deal for a sub $200 price range.

Still Not Convinced?

If you're still not convinced whether not the PowerSeeker is for you, here are some insightful statistics that might help:

  • Current Price: View on Amazon

  • Mount: Equatorial

  • Definite Price: Below $200

  • Aperature: 127mm

  • Focal Ratio: 127mm

Conclusion

Overall, for less than $200 dollars, the Celeston Powerseeker 127EQ is probably the best deal you'll get for beginner telescopes. Read more about the price on Amazon.
