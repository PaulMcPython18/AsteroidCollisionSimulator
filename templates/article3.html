1) 2017 PDC

Summary: According to a statement from the NASA Jet Propulsion Lab, Asteroid PDC 2017 will almost certainly hit earth in 2027. The exact date for this impact is July 21st, 2027. It is estimated that local populations could be affected by blast overpressure and tsunamis, not to mention the intial impact effects. Furthermore, when the asteroid flies over earth to its impact location, civilization under the asteroid would be severely affected by overpressure.

Diameter: 200 to 280 Meters

Could Hit Earth On: July 21st, 2027

Possible Effects: Overpressure and Tsunamis (Not including initial impact effects which would kill everything in a vicinity)

Percentage of Impact: 96% Chance of Impact (Almost Certain)

Fact: NASA has planned a research mission to rendezvous with Asteroid 2017 PDC.
