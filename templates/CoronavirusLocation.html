Steps: Follow the directions below to understand the Coronavirus Visualization Location Map!

1. View the coronavirus location map. Red circle sizes are marked with the severity of the location based upon the number of cases of coronavirus.

2. Hover over the location of the Coronavirus. When you hover over this location, a box will popup giving information about the certain Coronavirus. For example, the number of cases in the location/area, province/state, and country are included in these statistics.

Warning: Please consult a local health professional about Coronavirus Information. Do not take seriously any data provided on this map as data could be incorrect. Contact the CDC for more information.
